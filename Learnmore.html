Garissa faces recurring challenges that affect daily life.

This section highlights the key issues and practical solutions that can drive real, sustainable change.

Key Issues in Garissa

  • Water Scarcity: Limited access to clean and safe drinking water affects health and livelihoods. Solution: Implement community-based water management systems and invest in rainwater harvesting.
  • Waste Management: Inadequate waste disposal leads to environmental pollution. Solution: Establish organized waste collection services and promote recycling initiatives.
  • Youth Unemployment: High unemployment rates among youth contribute to social instability. Solution: Create vocational training programs and support local entrepreneurship.
  • Healthcare Access: Limited healthcare facilities hinder disease prevention and treatment. Solution: Expand healthcare infrastructure and mobile clinics to reach remote areas.
  • Education Quality: Insufficient educational resources affect learning outcomes. Solution: Invest in school infrastructure, teacher training, and provide learning materials.
